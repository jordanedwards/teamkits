About

Team Kits is based in Kelowna BC with direct-from-factory relationships with Macron, Joma, Inaria, Lotto and dealerships with Xara, New Balance (Warrior), Umbro, Mitre, Molten, Precision Training UK and others.

Team Kits focuses on providing a custom look for pro teams and competitive clubs but also delivers Value Kits for recreational clubs.

With exclusive relationships, Team Kits is targeting teams and clubs that are tired of looking like the teams they play against and are wanting their own unique look. As well, opportunity exists for pro teams that are looking to provide fanwear to their supporters and generate income to make their teams stronger

We look forward to working with clubs in the Pacific Northwest and across the USA and Canada.

The ordering process

  • 1

    Set up your account.

    Contact us to have your account set up. Once it is, you will be able to log in from this site and handle your orders.

  • 2

    Upload your logo and team colours.

    We will send your logo and team colours to the manufacturer and get a mockup made to suit your specifications.

  • 3

    Place your order

    We will upload an image of the custom order to your account. Give us the ok and we will send your jersey, shorts, socks, etc into production. We will generate your invoice and send it to you.

  • 4

    Proof your jersey

    We will upload an image of the custom order to your account. Give us the ok and we will send your jersey, shorts, socks, etc into production. We will generate your invoice and send it to you.
